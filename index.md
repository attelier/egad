**Errata Gathered, Appraised & Documented**

How Secur0's triage team scores the harder CVSS 4.0 calls, one vulnerability
class at a time. Each leaf holds the vectors for a class's realistic scenarios,
the per-metric reasoning, and where the line sits: why it isn't scored higher,
and the case where it would be.

Scores are derived from the vectors, never hand-typed. These are judgment calls,
made in the open and revised as we go.

The folder tree on the left is the taxonomy. Start with
[[injection/client_side/xss|Cross-Site Scripting]], or search. If you think a
call is wrong, bring a vector and a case. We do change our minds.
